Ramadan Dua for keeping a fast (Sehri ki Dua | Sehri Dua)

“Wa bisawmi ghaddan nawaiytu min shahri ramadan”
One more Dua is
“Navaitu an asuma gadalalilaahi ta aalla min fardi ramadan”

Ramadan Dua for breaking a fast (Iftar ki Dua | Iftar Dua)

“Allahumma inni laka sumtu wa bika aamantu wa 'alaykatawakkaltu wa 'ala rizq-ika aftarthu fathakabl minni”

رَّبِّ ٱغْفِرْ وَٱرْحَمْ وَأَنتَ خَيْرُ ٱلرَّٰحِمِينَ

“Oh my Lord, forgive and have mercy and You are the best of those who show mercy.”

رَبَّنَا فَٱغْفِرْ لَنَا ذُنُوبَنَا وَكَفِّرْ عَنَّاسَيِّـَٔاتِنَا وَتَوَفَّنَا مَعَ ٱلْأَبْرَارِ

“Lord! Therefore forgive us our sins, and remit from usour evil deeds, and make us die the death of the righteous”

اَللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنِي مِنَ النَّارِ

“O Allah! Save me from the hell's fire.”
